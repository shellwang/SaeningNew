About

We focus on litigation of most complex and high-stake intellectual property, antitrust, and commercial disputes. We specialize in dealing with patents, trade secrets, and licenses of cutting-edge technology, as well as issues concerning antitrust, unfair competition, data protection , commercial disputes and cross-border negotiations.

We have deep experiences in the areas of intellectual property, competition regulations, commercial litigation and arbitration. We have lawyers of diverse backgrounds that have been recognized for their battle tested experiences and results focused approaches.

We endeavor to provide prudent, effective and tailored services to all our clients, including multinational companies, domestic enterprises, and high-tech start ups. We are client oriented and focus on generating best and sustainable business impact.
