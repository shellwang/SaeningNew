Profile

Nan Li has served as a director in multiple departments in State Intellectual Property Office (SIPO) for more than 10 years, and was appointed as one of the first batch of technical experts of the Beijing Municipal Intellectual Property Office, as well as one of the first batch of People's Jurors (Expert Juror) of the Beijing Intellectual Property Court.


Nan Li has worked with the Patent Examination Cooperation Beijing Center of SIPO since 2005, she is devoted to patent examination in the field of internet, IT hardware and software, e-commerce, telecommunication, network communication, blockchain, artificial intelligence, big data, medical devices, high-end manufacturing and so on. As the director of the Quality Control Office, she was responsible for conducting analysis for the overall quality of cases, analyzing complex cases, unifying the examination standards and public responses to key complaints. In Patent Service Department, Nan Li was in charge of overall management of patent service projects, during which she once cooporated with enterprises, universities and local intellectual property offices. She participated in a broad range of national-level projects such as “Internet of Things Industry Innovation” for Guangdong Province, and "Research on Development and Plan of Independent Intellectual Property for the Standard Bullet Train", etc.


As a technical expert juror and a technical analyst, Nan Li has participated in patent litigation and administrative law enforcement proceedings in the field of Computing, Telecom, including cases with major social influence, like, Apple Inc. v. Shanghai Zhisheng Co. (involving Apple "Siri" technology), Huawei v. ZTE series patent invalidation cases, the administrative procedure of patent infringement between Jinyun Co. and Mobike Inc. (the first patent infringement case with regard to sharing bicycle technology), and the administrative case of patent re-examination of a cell gene company, etc.


Nan Li was selected by SIPO as a Tutor for patent attorney training, responsible for developing the courses of patent infringement and innovation for SIPO. She was in charge of the training courses for patent agents for the SIPO (regarding the drafting of application documents in the electrical field), advanced training on operation of SIPO, training course of new examiners for the Beijing Center of Patent Examination Cooperation, the course of patent for innovation of medical devices of Food and Drug Administration, and the Patent Popularization Course of Tsinghua University, etc.

Moreover, Nan Li has co-authored three books: “Analysis of Typical Cases of Invention Patent Protection Object”, “Patent Search Strategy and Application” and “The Invention World of Steve Jobs”. She has participated in 13 research projects and published more than 10 thesis.
