Profile

Feifei Feng has extensive experience in dealing with domestic and cross-border complex commercial litigation and arbitration. During her more than ten years of practice, Feifei has handled many complex commercial litigation for international companies and domestic enterprises, and appeared before court as lead counsel in many cases. Her areas of practice include trade secret, patent, trademark, anti-trust and unfair competition, software copyright, data protection and technology transactions, etc.


Feifei has recently acted for some of the world's leading companies including Micron Technology Inc., Broadcom Inc., Milliken & Company, Moody's Corporation, Citrix Systems and Hikvision, involving a wide range of industries, including semiconductors, wireless communications, textiles, LCD glass substrate, coal chemicals, pesticide, chemical compounds and production methods, medical equipment, software and systems, etc.


Feifei formerly worked at Jones Day Hong Kong, Jun He Law Offices and Zhong Lun Law Firm.
