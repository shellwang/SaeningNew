Profile

Carol entered Peking University Law School in 2006 with the second place in the Shandong province university entrance examination and acquired her bachelor degree in law and a master degree in intellectual property law at Peking University. Carol was exchanged to Stanford University School of Law for a copyright law and patent law program in 2011.


Carol Cai began to practice in 2012. Since then, she has engaged in litigation, arbitration and other dispute resolutions. She has participated in handling a series of complex commercial lawsuits involving many transnational corporations and large domestic enterprises. Carol mainly focuses on patent litigation, trade secret infringement cases, product liability and contractual disputes. Many of these cases involve complex technical fields, including anti- cancer targeted drugs, seed coating agents, automotive active safety systems (electronic control devices and tire pressure warning devices in brake systems such as ABS, ESP, EBD, OBD, etc.), lithium sub-batteries, and large transformers for substations, etc.


Before joining Saelink Law, Carol has worked at Jun He Law Offices and Fen Xun Law Firm for more than six years.
